1. General Information

UPLOADER++ is a browser extension that enhances the audio upload experience on Roblox, including spoofer, fast generation, and intro/audio tools. All audio processing is performed locally on your device. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Local Storage

  • Extension settings (theme, language, upload preferences)
  • Upload history (stored only on your device)
  • Audio processing data (processed entirely on your device via FFmpeg WASM, never sent to external servers)

3. Use of Data

  • Provide extension functionality
  • Process audio locally on your device (spoofer, fast generation, intro/audio)
  • Save your preferences and upload history
  • Improve user experience

4. Security

  • All audio processing happens locally on your device â€” no audio is sent to external servers
  • No personal data, passwords, or credentials are collected or stored remotely
  • The extension only communicates with Roblox APIs (create.roblox.com and apis.roblox.com) for uploads
  • All communication with Roblox uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

5. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. No data leaves your device except audio files uploaded directly to Roblox through its official API.

6. Extension Permissions

  • Storage: To save settings and upload history locally
  • Tabs: To detect when you're on Roblox Create
  • Host permissions: To interact only with create.roblox.com and apis.roblox.com
  • Offscreen: To run audio processing (FFmpeg WASM) in the background without performance throttling
  • Downloads: To save processed audio files to your device

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can clear it by clearing the extension's storage or uninstalling the extension.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The UPLOADER++ extension does not use tracking cookies, analytics, or any form of telemetry. We only use browser local storage to save your preferences.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We recommend reviewing this page occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension.
