1. General Information

UPLOADER++ is a browser extension that enhances the audio upload experience on Roblox. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Local Storage

  • Extension settings (theme, upload preferences)
  • Upload history (stored only on your device)

3. Use of Data

  • Provide extension functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Communicate important updates

4. Security

  • We do not store Roblox passwords or credentials
  • Licenses are device-bound to prevent unauthorized sharing
  • Servers protected with security best practices

5. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to process payments (via Stripe) or when required by law.

6. Extension Permissions

  • Storage: To save settings and history locally
  • Tabs: To detect when you're on Roblox Create
  • Host permissions: To interact only with create.roblox.com and apis.roblox.com
  • Downloads: To save processed audio files

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us via Discord or email.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The UPLOADER++ extension does not use tracking cookies. We only use browser local storage to save your preferences.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We recommend reviewing this page occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension.
