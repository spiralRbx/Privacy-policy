This Privacy Policy describes how Audio Converter ("the Extension") handles information when you use it. By installing or using the Extension, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The Extension does not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or share any information that could identify you or any other person.

The only data stored is:

Key: "theme"  |  Value: "dark" or "light"
Location: chrome.storage.local (on your device only)
Purpose: Remember your UI theme preference between sessions

This preference data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

2. Your Audio Files

Audio files you convert are processed entirely within your browser using WebAssembly (FFmpeg.wasm). This means:

  • Your files are never uploaded to any server.
  • Your files are never transmitted over the internet.
  • Your files are never stored outside your device's memory.
  • Converted files are offered as a browser download, directly to your device.

Once you close the tab or navigate away, all file data is cleared from memory. The Extension has no persistent access to your file system.

3. Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Why it is required What it does NOT allow
storage Save your dark/light theme preference locally between sessions. Cannot access your Google account data, sync data, or any other extensions' storage.

No other permissions are requested. The Extension does not access your browsing history, tabs, cookies, clipboard, camera, microphone, or any other browser APIs beyond those explicitly listed.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or remote APIs.

The only external resource loaded is the Google Fonts stylesheet (fonts.googleapis.com), used to display the interface typography. This is a standard CDN request and does not include any user data or file content. If you prefer fully offline operation, the font will fall back to system fonts automatically when there is no internet connection.

5. Offline Operation

After FFmpeg.wasm is loaded for the first time (a one-time ~31 MB download cached by the browser), the Extension operates completely offline. No internet connection is required for audio conversion.

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because we collect no personal data from any user, the Extension is safe for general use.

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will publish the new version on this page and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
